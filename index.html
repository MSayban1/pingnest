Privacy Policy

At Ping Nest, we prioritize your privacy and security. Our platform is designed to provide a safe and secure environment for global communication while respecting your privacy rights.

Key Privacy Points:

  • No Email Required: We don't collect or store your email address. You can start chatting immediately without providing any personal contact information.
  • No Message Monitoring: We don't monitor, read, or store your private conversations. Your messages are transmitted securely and are not subject to surveillance.
  • Don't Harass Anyone: We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, bullying, or abusive behavior. Users who violate this policy will be immediately removed from the platform.

We collect only the minimal information necessary to provide our service: your chosen username, country (auto-detected), and IP address for connection purposes. This information is used solely for chat functionality and is not shared with third parties.

By using Ping Nest, you agree to treat all users with respect and maintain a positive, harassment-free environment for everyone.
